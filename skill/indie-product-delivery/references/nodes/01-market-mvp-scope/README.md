# Market and MVP Scope

Node01 owns product truth: target user and buyer, problem, promise, first success, pricing, positioning, MVP/MAP boundary, validation, and acceptance. Use it only when one of those facts is the present decision; do not route an approved local implementation here.

| Need | Read |
|---|---|
| recover decisions, calibrate ambition, cut scope, write acceptance, control a changed requirement | `product-discovery-scope-and-acceptance.md` |
| validate demand, alternatives, pricing, distribution, positioning, trust, or public visual meaning | `market-validation-positioning-and-trust.md` |

Enter with the user request and any repository, customer, market, or existing product evidence. Separate observed facts, inferences, reversible defaults, and founder decisions. If implementation is requested before demand is known, either perform proportionate low-cost validation or mark the slice assumption-led; do not present absence of research as confidence.

Node01 has enough product truth for a handoff when the relevant actor, outcome, first-success path, kept and excluded scope, acceptance, and material business constraints are explicit. A pricing, promise, entitlement, audience, or primary-workflow change remains a product decision even when it appears inside a technical request.

Route stable product truth to Node02 only when a system or contract decision is required. Route approved implementation directly to Node03 or Node04. Use `references/agent-context-html/instructions.md` when product truth must become durable project documentation.
