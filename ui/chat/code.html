Can you summarize the findings from the Q3 clinical trial reports and cross-reference them with recent publications on mRNA stability?

Based on the hybrid analysis, the Q3 clinical trial reports indicate a 14% improvement in overall structural integrity when stored at -80°C compared to previous iterations [1]. This aligns closely with recent findings from the web.

However, the internal reports note a faster degradation rate during the thawing process (noted on pg 15 of Q3_Trial_Results_v2.pdf), which external literature suggests might be mitigated by adjusting the lipid molar ratios [2].
