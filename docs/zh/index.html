把证据审阅做成可检查的 AI 系统。

ResearchMate 是一套面向多源资料审阅的 Agentic RAG 工作流。它将限定范围内的资料整理为主张、引文、决策与报告修订，并把接口约束、测试证据和仍未验证的运行时边界如实摆在读者面前。

Why this project exists

An engineering portfolio, not a commercial SaaS

Portfolio objective

Demonstrate AI full-stack architecture, durable workflows, retrieval quality, data contracts, observability, failure recovery, security boundaries, and CI/CD with evidence an interviewer can inspect.

Explicitly outside the objective

Customer acquisition, revenue, pricing, subscriptions, billing, CRM, marketing automation, and growth analytics are not success criteria and will not drive the roadmap.

Reader action: recruiters can assess scope quickly; engineers can follow the linked contracts and evidence; demo users exercise system behavior rather than a conversion funnel.

The scenario that earns the stack

From a source collection to an auditable evidence report

A simple document chatbot would not justify the target architecture. The committed scenario coordinates long-running ingestion, parallel evidence work, stateful review, incremental recomputation, and measurable quality.

01 / INGESTBuild the source corpusUpload tens to hundreds of documents and web sources; parse, deduplicate, version, chunk, and index them asynchronously.
02 / PLANDecompose the research goalLangGraph turns a broad question into bounded sub-questions and persists checkpoints for interruption and resume.
03 / EXTRACTBuild an evidence modelCelery workers retrieve in parallel, extract claims, attach page-level citations, and classify support, conflict, duplication, and gaps.
04 / REVIEWResolve uncertaintyLow-confidence conclusions, untrusted web evidence, and prompt-injection signals pause for an explicit human decision.
05 / SYNTHESIZEPublish and update the reportGenerate a cited report and recompute only affected claims and sections when the corpus changes.
06 / PROVEEvaluate the systemCompare retrieval and pipeline versions; inspect traces, latency, tokens, cost, retries, failures, and recovery.
